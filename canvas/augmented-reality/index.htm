Augmented Reality Piano (Part 1)

We learned to layer the canvases
so that we have an AR Piano between
the user's hands and the background.

We learned to detect the difference
between two images and hide similar
pixels by changing their alpha.

We learned how to enhance the button
handler developed in lecture 4 to 
support pixel based hit testing.

We learned to make a first variant of 
the AR Piano :-)
